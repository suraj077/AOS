Task 4

You will hear(Track No. CBSE 2.4) a speech about joint families. Read the questions given, then listen to the extract and choose option (a) or (b) or (c) for each question. You will hear the recording twice.

1. People prefer nuclear families because:

  1. there is more freedom.
  2. there are fewer members.
  3. it is the latest trend in many countries.

2. According to the speaker, young people don't listen to elders because they:

  1. don't want to be restricted.
  2. think they know better.
  3. learn from their mistakes.

3. In a joint family, you don't have to ask for the help from outsides as:

  1. you are smart enough to make decisions.
  2. your childern are able to take care of themselves.
  3. your family members are there to support you.

4. Joint families follow the concept of sharing as:

  1. the clothes are shared among family members.
  2. all family members are collectively responsible.
  3. the finances are distributed equally.

Joint families do not depend on outside help for childern because:

  1. grandparents take care of them.
  2. mothers stay at home.
  3. childern care for each other.

The best way to create a good joint family is to:

  1. be open to differences in opinion.
  2. listen to elders all the time.
  3. not voice your views on anything.
