General Instructions for Students:

You are not allowed to ask questions or interrupt the Examiner at any point.

  • You are being tested on your Listening Skills.
  • You will hear a set of recordings of the Listening input or listen to a reading of the listening input.
  • Each of the recording will be played twice. In case there is an oral rendering of the Listening Input, it shall take place twice.
  • You are required to answer a set of questions based on each of the Listening Inputs.
  • The assessment consists of 4 tasks.
  • You are required to attempt all 4 tasks of the assessment.
  • Familiarize yourself with the questions on thw worksheet. it will help you to answer them later on.
  • After you have listened to the input, you will be given specified minutes to answer the questions on your worksheet.
  • You may answer the questions on your worksheet while listening.
  • Do not interact or comment until you have moved out of the Examination Room.
Remember: The only time we learn is when our ears are open, and our mouths closed.
